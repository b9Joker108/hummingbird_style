Śithilīkaraṇa Vyāyāma (शिथिलीकरण व्यायाम)

- Devanagari: शिथिलीकरण व्यायाम  
- IAST: śithīlīkaraṇa vyāyāma  
- Lay pronunciation: shi-thee-lee-ka-ra-na vya-yaa-ma  
- Academic pronunciation: [ɕiːt̪ʰiːliːkəɾəɳə ʋjaːjaːmə]  
- Root components:  
  • śithīla (शिथीला) “slack, loose” from √śithī “to be slack”  
  • karaṇa (करण) “making, causing” from √kṛ “to do”  
  • vyāyāma (व्यायाम) “exercise, exertion” from vi- “apart” + ā “toward” + yam “stretch”  
- Etymology: “exercises causing slackening”  
- Semantic field: loosen; slacken; relax; release; unbind; mobilize; soften; unfix  
- Denotation: a systematic warm-up of every joint and limb before entering asana proper

Aṅga-Saṃcālana-Kriyā (अङ्गसञ्चालनक्रिया)

- Devanagari: अङ्गसञ्चालनक्रिया  
- IAST: aṅga-saṃcālana-kriyā  
- Lay pronunciation: ung-ga sun-chaa-la-na kri-yaa  
- Academic pronunciation: [ə̃ŋɡə sə̃t͡ʃaːləna kɾiːjaː]  
- Root components:  
  • aṅga (अङ्ग) “limb, part of the body” from √aṅ “to adhere, limb”  
  • saṃcālana (सञ्चालन) “movement, mobilization” from sam “together” + cālana “moving”  
  • kriyā (क्रिया) “action, practice” from √kṛ “to do”  
- Etymology: “limb-mobilization practices”  
- Semantic field: articulation; segmental movement; joint motion; part-wise mobilization; oscillation; rotation; flexion; extension  
- Denotation: the sequence of joint-by-joint movements that primes each articulation

Substages of Aṅga-Saṃcālana-Kriyā

1. Gulpha-Saṃcālana (गुल्फसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: गुल्फसञ्चालन  
- IAST: gulpha-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: gul-fah sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [ɡuɭpʰə sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • gulpha (गुल्फ) “ankle” possibly from √guh “to cover” (the joint that ‘covers’ the foot)  
  • saṃcālana “movement” (see above)  
- Semantic field: ankle rotation; plantarflexion; dorsiflexion; circumduction; foot articulation  
- Denotation: clockwise & counterclockwise rotations and flexion–extension to lubricate the ankle  

2. Jānū-Saṃcālana (जानुसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: जानुसञ्चालन  
- IAST: jānū-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: jaa-noo sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [d͡ʒaːnuː sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • jānū (जानु) “knee” from √jan “to be born” (the primary hinge of the leg)  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: knee flexion; knee extension; slight rotation; hinge articulation; ligament play  
- Denotation: gentle bend-straighten cycles with micro-rotations to prime the knee joint  

3. Ūru-Saṃcālana (ऊरुसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: ऊरुसञ्चालन  
- IAST: ūru-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: oo-roo sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [uːru sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • ūru (ऊरु) “thigh, hip region” from √ur “to cover, spread”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: hip circles; thigh swings; femoral circumduction; hip protraction; hip retraction  
- Denotation: hands-on-hips pendulum swings and circles to free the hip articulation  

4. Kaṭi-Saṃcālana (कटिसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: कटिसञ्चालन  
- IAST: kaṭi-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: ka-ti sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [kəʈi sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • kaṭi (कटी) “waist, lumbar” from √kaṭ “to bind”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: lumbar twists; side bends; waist flexion; extension; torso articulation  
- Denotation: standing or seated waist rotations and lateral bends to mobilize the lower spine  

5. Meru-Saṃcālana (मेरुसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: मेरुसञ्चालन  
- IAST: meru-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: may-roo sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [meːɾu sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • meru (मेरु) “spine, central axis” from √mr “to be situated”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: vertebral undulations; cat-cow movements; spinal articulations; column flexion/extension  
- Denotation: coordinated flexion and extension of the entire spinal column  

6. Vakṣa-Saṃcālana (वक्षसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: वक्षसञ्चालन  
- IAST: vakṣa-saṃcālana

- Academic pronunciation: [ʋəkʂə sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]

  • vakṣa (वक्ष) “chest, thorax” from √vṛkṣ “to spread”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: rib expansions; thoracic twists; chest openings; breath mobilization  
- Denotation: seated or standing rib-cage rotations and spreads to free the upper torso  

7. Skandha-Saṃcālana (स्कन्धसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: स्कन्धसञ्चालन  
- IAST: skandha-saṃcālana

- Root components:  
  • skandha (स्कन्ध) “shoulder, upper arm” from √skand “to ascend, pile”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: shoulder rolls; scapular slides; circumduction; protraction; retraction  
- Denotation: circular and sliding movements of the shoulder girdle  

8. Bāhu-Saṃcālana (बाहुसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: बाहुसञ्चालन  
- IAST: bāhu-saṃcālana

- Academic pronunciation: [baːɦu sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • bāhu (बाहु) “arm” from √bhuj “to hold, carry”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: elbow flexion; elbow extension; forearm pronation; forearm supination  
- Denotation: gentle hinge and rotary motions at the elbow and upper arm  

9. Hasta-Saṃcālana (हस्तसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: हस्तसञ्चालन  
- IAST: hasta-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: hus-ta sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [ɦəst̪ə sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • hasta (हस्त) “hand” from √hast “to seize”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: wrist rotations; radial deviation; ulnar deviation; finger spreads; hand flexion/extension  
- Denotation: circling and bending movements of the wrist and hand  

10. Grīva-Saṃcālana (ग्रीवसञ्चालन)

- Devanagari: ग्रीवसञ्चालन  
- IAST: grīva-saṃcālana  
- Lay pronunciation: gree-va sun-chaa-la-na  
- Academic pronunciation: [ɡriːʋə sə̃t͡ʃaːləna]  
- Root components:  
  • grīva (ग्रीव) “neck” from √grī “to twist”  
  • saṃcālana “movement”  
- Semantic field: neck flexion; neck extension; lateral bends; rotations; cervical articulation  
- Denotation: slow, mindful rotations and bends of the neck to prepare the cervical spine
