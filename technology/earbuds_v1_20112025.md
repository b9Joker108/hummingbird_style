### Secure and Comfortable Fit for Dynamic Movement
For bodywork involving inversions and handstands, earbuds must not only fit in-ear but stay securely in place under shifting positions and movements. The best earbuds for active use offer a combination of ergonomic design, multiple ear tip sizes, flexible earhooks or stabilizer wings, and sometimes specialized locking mechanisms. ### Durability and Sweat/Water Resistance
Exercise earbuds must resist sweat, moisture, and some environmental exposure. Reliable SOTA earbuds use robust materials (reinforced plastics, corrosion-resistant metals) and have ratings such as IPX4 or higher to indicate water resistance. The TREBLAB X3-PRO and Shokz OpenRun Pro 2, both highly recommended for workouts, offer IPX5 and IP55 ratings respectively, ensuring good protection against sweat and light water exposure during prolonged active sessions [1][5][2][3].

Earbuds tailored for exercise often include easily accessible physical or touch controls adapted for quick use without disrupting workout flow, volume adjustments, and voice assistant compatibility.
